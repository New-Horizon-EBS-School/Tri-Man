About Cyber Security

Cyber security is the process of using best practices to protect computers, servers, systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks such as viruses, worms, ransomware, and other threats aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive data, extorting money, or interrupting normal business processes.
